Scroll wheel

Requires jquery.mousewheel.js (included). Degrades gracefully.

  • H - Use the scroll wheel over the wheel while holding shift to adjust the Hue.
  • S - Use the scroll wheel over the wheel to adjust the Saturation.
  • V - Use the scroll wheel over the slider to adjust the Value.
